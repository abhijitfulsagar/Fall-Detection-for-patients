The Challenge of Wandering

Of the many faces of Alzheimer's disease, wandering is the most hazardous. As a Caregiver, you may encounter bouts of chaos and uncertainty while striving to keep your loved one safe and independent-to live with joy, dignity, and self-respect. For the one living with Alzheimier's, memory los, confusion, delusion and wandering are but a few of the scenarios they'll encounter. As a Caregiver, redirection and capitulation are tools you'll learn to embrace. But you don't have the tools to cope with wandering. That's where we come in.

The Solution - FindMe

FindMe helps you keep your promises to your loved one by adding your virtual presence when you can't be around. We help you find the one who has wandered to bring them home.

Our research has included the Banner's Alzheimer's Institute, Brookdale, Emeritus, Caregivers, and Gerontologist, resulting in the development of a sophisticated and non-untrusive bi-modal rescue beacon, and a product configuration created explicitly for Alzheimer's. With FindMe, you can be there even when you can't
