At AlzCare Labs, we focus on one question

Why would this woman's

family move her...

...to here?

She wandered, and

she has Alzheimer's.

And it made them

afraid.

So, we asked, how can we

help her remain in her home.

That she loves.

Unconfined?

Build her a virtual dementia village with FindMe

There's one person with ADOD for every 15 housing units in San Diego County

FindMe will keep her safe, and her family unafraid

Community Respite, Rescue, and Support

Hazard Alerts for Exposure, Traffic, Drowning and Falling

Peace of mind that comes from always knowing where to find her

The technology behind FindMe: Device + Mobile app

Temperature sensor detects weather danger, and change in location (patent pending)

Accelerometer detects falls, change in location, distance

Li-ion battery provides up to 7 weeks of power

Barometric Pressure sensor detects floor levels in tall buildings, immediate water risk


Telit CDMA modem (GSM in EU and Asia) offers trilateration and uses the established IS-95 standard for maximum performance in difficult windowless and dense material environments

GNSS receiver supports worldwide coverage using GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and COMPASS systems

We took the concept of the ocean-going EPIRB [Emergency Position Indicating Radio Beacon], and put it into a domino-sized safety/rescue beacon for cell towers and land
